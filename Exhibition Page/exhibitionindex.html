Pop Art

The 60s brought colour and vibrancy and this was reflected in the movement of Pop Art where simple recognisable objects were given centre stage in works of art such as James Rosenquist and Roy Lichtenstein. Most famous examples of artists in this category are Andy Warhol and David Hockney.
